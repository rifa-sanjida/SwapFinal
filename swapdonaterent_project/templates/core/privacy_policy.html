Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, phone number, and address.
  • Profile Information: You may provide additional information like your bio and profile picture.
  • Contact Information: Information you provide when contacting other users through our messaging system.

Item Information

  • Details about items you post for swapping, donating, or renting
  • Photos of items you upload
  • Location information for item pickup/delivery

Usage Information

  • How you use our platform
  • Items you view or add to your cart
  • Messages you send and receive

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To facilitate communication between users
  • To display items and user profiles
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To send important updates and notifications
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Other Users

  • Your username and profile information are visible to other users
  • Items you post are visible to all platform users
  • Contact information you provide in item listings is visible to interested users

Service Providers

  • We may use third-party services to help operate our platform
  • These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements

Legal Requirements

  • If required by law or to protect our rights
  • To comply with legal processes
  • To protect the safety of our users

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view and update your profile information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data
  • Correction: You can correct inaccurate information
  • Objection: You can object to certain data processing

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information is removed
  • Your items are deleted
  • Your messages may be retained for a limited time for legal purposes

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve our services

You can control cookies through your browser settings.
